Researching quantum phenomena at the nanoscale

Welcome to my academic website. I investigate how local perturbations affect the magneto-electronic and optoelectronic properties of low-dimensional quantum dots using first-principles density functional theory.

Research Focus

My work explores confined molecular systems to understand spin-dependent responses to small perturbations-effects that are significantly amplified compared to bulk materials. The goal is to develop multifunctional nanodots for spintronic devices, quantum sensing technologies, and quantum information processing. By establishing predictive frameworks and quantitative design principles, I aim to inform the development of energy-efficient, highly sensitive, and secure information technologies that address future societal and computational demands.
