Play a Game

I made a simple HTML-based online chess game named SpinMate, that uses various spin operators as playing elements and follows quantum mechanical rules to capture pieces. This program is only a few days old and still underdeveloped. Anyone is welcome to contribute to its development if they find it interesting. Here is the repository where relevant documentation can be found: https://github.com/sarafmohaimen/SpinMate
